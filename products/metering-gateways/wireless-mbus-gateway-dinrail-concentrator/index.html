  • Product Name: Wireless M-Bus Gateway V3 (Battery)
  • Product Type: LOB-GW-HYB-WMBUS

Descriptionâ€‹

Battery-powered gateway for regular remote reading of up to 650...800 wireless M-Bus (868 MHz) and Sensus RF consumption meters (e.g. water meters, heat cost allocators) via NB-IoT, LTE-M mobile radio or LoRaWAN to the Internet. Suitable for indoor and weather-protected outdoor installation. Ideal for intra-year consumption information (uVI) and billing in the housing industry.
